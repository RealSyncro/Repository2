Oceania Distoria: Product Showcase Livestream scheduled on 28 July

BP Social Media:

Blueprint Studios announces "Oceania Distoria" theme for new products showcase.
Livestream timings are as follows: 15:00 (GMT+8) to 17:00 (GMT+8).

With summer on the rise this season, we are proud to announce to you: "Oceania Distoria", as the current theme for the new product showcase stream! The live stream will feature several exquisite models that you may find fascinating or jaw-dropping! It will mainly adhered to the scorching hot summer theme, so expect there be objects or models related to it. In the live stream, Mr Kolarov, the CEO of Newton Studio, will also be here as a guest!

Before you click off this page, we'll also be holding a giveaway at the end of the livestream! The giveaway includes car models, skyscraper models, furniture models and many more! Be sure to tune into the livestream to not miss the giveaway!

As the anniversary for Blueprint Studios inches ever closer towards us, we will be announcing the details of the anniversary stream soon. Do check the news here regularly to stay up-to-date with regards to the anniversary stream.
