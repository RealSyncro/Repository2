Benefits and Perks at Blueprint Studio

We want to empower and inspire our team members to do their best work every day - making sure they feel happy, healthy and fulfilled both inside and
outside of the workplace.


Competitive Pay Take-what-you-need paid vacation policy Employee Benefits
We offer a competitive total rewards package, where every employee is an owner of our stock. We encourage employees to find a comfortable work-life balance by taking as many days off as they need while still being able to perform their jobs satisfactorily. (We really mean it!) We offer a comprehensive benefits package including healthcare, life insurance, short- and long-term disability, pension plans in accordance with the market practice in our locations. A complimentary global program that aims to provide family forming and mental wellbeing support to our employees across our offices.

Paid maternity and paternity leave Commuter program
Our global parental leave policy allows up to sixteen paid weeks of bonding leave time for all qualifying new parents. We are flexible about where we work and value connecting at the office. Our commuter benefits program is in place to support team members' commute to work via public transportation without the extra cost burden.
